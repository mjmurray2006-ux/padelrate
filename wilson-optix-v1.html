Verdict

The Wilson Optix V1 is a purpose-built beginner racket from one of the most trusted names in racket sports. Its round shape, oversized head and low-density foam core combine to deliver maximum forgiveness and comfort for players just starting their padel journey.

Full Review

Wilson has over a century of experience making racket sports equipment, and the Optix V1 shows that expertise applied to the beginner padel market. This is a racket designed with one clear objective: making padel as accessible and enjoyable as possible for new players.

The round shape with an oversized head is the defining characteristic. The large hitting surface expands the sweet spot significantly, meaning off-centre contacts still produce usable shots. For beginners who haven't yet developed consistent timing, this translates directly into more successful rallies and a more rewarding experience on court.

Low-density foam at the core keeps the feel soft and forgiving. The ball doesn't ping off aggressively, which helps beginners control placement rather than fighting the racket. The fiberglass frame adds flexibility and comfort, reducing the vibration that can fatigue the arm and wrist during practice sessions.

Sharp Hole Technology — Wilson's advanced hole-drilling technique — enhances bite and grip on the ball with every stroke. Even with a beginner's technique, this adds a small but noticeable improvement in spin and control, making it easier to keep the ball in the court.

At around £74, the Optix V1 sits at an accessible entry point for a Wilson racket. The brand credibility, combined with the genuinely well-thought-out design, makes it a confident recommendation for anyone just picking up padel for the first time.

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Oversized round head maximises the sweet spot
  • Low-density foam is very comfortable on the arm
  • Sharp Hole Technology improves grip and spin
  • Trusted Wilson brand with great build quality

Cons

  • Very limited power — purely a beginner's racket
  • Will need replacing as skills develop
  • Low-density foam less responsive than EVA alternatives

Best For

Complete beginners who want maximum forgiveness and comfort from a trusted brand to start learning padel properly.
