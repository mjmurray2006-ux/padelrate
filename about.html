PadelRate was built out of frustration. When I was looking for my first serious padel racket, every site I found was either full of generic marketing copy, had no idea what they were talking about, or was just a thin Amazon affiliate page dressed up as a review site.

I'm a university student in the UK and a padel player. I started the sport a couple of years ago and quickly became obsessed — not just with playing, but with the gear. When I started spending real money on rackets, I wanted honest information. So I decided to build the site I wished existed.

PadelRate combines hands-on player experience, community feedback from other UK players, and detailed spec analysis to give you ratings you can actually trust. I test rackets personally where I can, and I'm transparent when a review draws on spec analysis and community data rather than direct play.

How We Score Rackets

Power (0–100): How much punch and depth the racket generates, factoring in balance point, frame shape, and core density.

Control (0–100): The precision and predictability of the racket — how easy it is to place the ball exactly where you want it.

Touch (0–100): The feel and feedback at contact, particularly relevant at the net for volleys and bandeja shots.

The Overall Score is a weighted combination of all three, adjusted for how the racket performs relative to others in its price and level category.
