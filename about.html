About PadelRate

Independent, honest padel racket reviews. No brand deals, no bias.

Who We Are

PadelRate is run by padel players, for padel players. We test and rate rackets based on real-world performance so you can make a smarter buying decision — whether you're just starting out or competing at club level.

We cover every major brand in the UK market including Bullpadel, Head, NOX, Babolat, Adidas, Oxdog and more.

How We Score Rackets

Every racket is given an overall score out of 100, plus individual scores for three key dimensions:

Power
0–100
Raw speed and pace generation from smashes and drives
Control
0–100
Precision and consistency in placement and direction
Touch
0–100
Feel, feedback and softness at the net and on drops

Scores take into account frame materials, core type, shape, balance point, and real-world playability testing. We also consider value for money at each price point.

Why Trust Us?

We have no sponsorship deals with racket manufacturers and don't accept free rackets in exchange for positive reviews. Our goal is simple: give you the most honest, useful racket information on the internet.

Affiliate Links

Some links on PadelRate are affiliate links. If you click through and buy a racket, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This helps us keep the site running and free to use. It never influences our ratings or recommendations — we only recommend rackets we genuinely rate.
